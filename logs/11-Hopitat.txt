
Denny Creek Hopitat IPA
10 gallons


26 lb American 2 row
1  lb 10L Crystal Malt
1  lb 20L Crystal Malt

4 oz Magnum 11.5% - 75 minutes
4 oz Centennial - 0 minutes
4 oz Centennial - dry hop in kegs

1098 British Ale™ yeast


Mash at 150° to 152° F for 60 min.
Sparge with 165°-175° water to obtain about 13.5 gallons of wort
Boil for 75 minutes
Ferment at 66° to 68° F


Mash water quantity: 28 lb. grain X 1.25 quarts/lb. = 35 quarts = ~ 9 gallons
Target total wort: 10 boiled wort + .5 boiled hops absorption + 3.5 boiloff = 14 gallons
Sparge water: 14 - 9 + 2 mash absorbtion = 7 gallons
Total water quantity: 9 + 7 = 16 gallons

Strike water temperature:
Tw = (.2/r)(T2-T1)+T2
Tw = (.2/1.25)(152-50)+152
Tw = .16 X 102 + 152
Tw = 168° F
