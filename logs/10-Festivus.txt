
Denny Creek Festivus Ale
10 gallons


26 lb 2 row (Great Western)
2  lb 120L Crystal (Breiss)
1  lb 60L Crystal (Breiss)

2 oz Chinook 60 min
2 oz Centennial 45 min
2 oz Centennial 30 min
2 oz Centennial 15 min
2 oz Cascade while cooling
2 oz Cascade dry
2 oz Centennial dry

Wyeast 1056 American Ale™ yeast


Mash at 152° F for 90 minutes
Sparge with 165°-175° F water to obtain about 13.75 gallons of wort
Boil for 75 minutes
Add hops according to schedule


Mash water quantity: 29 lb. grain X 1.25 quarts/lb. = ~36 quarts = 9 gallons
Target total wort: 9.5 boiled wort + .75 boiled hops absorption + 3.5 boiloff = 13.75 gallons
Sparge water: 13.75 - 9 + 2 mash absorbtion = 6.75 gallons
Total water quantity: 9 + 6.75 = 15.75 gallons

Strike water temperature:
Tw = (.2/r)(T2-T1)+T2
Tw = (.2/1.25)(152-45)+152
Tw = .16 X 107 + 152
Tw = 169.1° F
